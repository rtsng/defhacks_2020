Grace: I absolutely loved this place! We decided to come here on a whim and my family definitely enjoyed their time here. The children had fun swimming while the rest of us relaxed on the beach. Will definitely be coming back next year! John: I really miss living in High Park. My second year of living in this city, I lived in a condo that overlooked the entire park facing south and every morning I would wake up to the sight of green (white if it was winter) and the blue horizon of the lake. High Park is the best park in Toronto to spend a whole day in. Take your kids, take your dog, take your mom. There are many kms of trails that zig zag your way through the park and you might even come on to some hidden gems that you didn't know about. Like the mini zoo (though now closed due to the pandemic) known for its notorious escaping jailbirds (hah- pun intended) like the screaming peacock screaming its lungs out as it roamed the neighborhood for weeks before the authorities caught up with him. Or the pair of Capybaras that made the lake inside the park their vacation resort. The park is also the best place to catch Cherry Blossoms every year in spring, however I doubt you'll see many this year due to the social distancing restrictions. Plan to spend the day there, just take a picnic basket, some blankets, some wine and water and you will not regret getting lost in all the green and forget that you are dead centre of a bustling city. PS. It's a great place to take engagement pictures as well! Steven: Well, this is my hood. Definitely my number one park in Toronto. Ideal for walking and for hanging out with friends and family. High Park is home for many festivals and activities during the year. If you are visiting the park on the weekends during spring and summer months it is not easy to find a parking spot. You can enter the park from a few sides but the main entrance is from Bloor Street West. There is also Zoo inside the park with free admission. The Grenadier Cafe is located in the heart of the park so if you wanna coffee or some food this place is good for it. It is very expensive but they have a cool location inside the park with a nice patio area in front. If you like fishing there are some cool spots for that. Cherry blossom is one of my favorite festivals in High Park. Keep in mind if you are planning to visit this festival it is very crowded and it is impossible to find the parking.
